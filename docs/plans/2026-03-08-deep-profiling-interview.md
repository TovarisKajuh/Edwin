# Deep Profiling Interview — Seeding Edwin's Mind

> **Purpose:** Collect the real, unfiltered truth about Jan's life so Edwin can operate as a genuine companion from day one — not spend weeks learning basics through conversation.
>
> **Approach:** Slow, conversational, one section at a time. No rushing. Each answer gets stored as high-confidence `told` observations in Edwin's memory.
>
> **Rule:** We want the REAL version, not the aspirational one. Edwin needs to know who Jan IS, not who he wants to be. The aspirational version is already in the vision.

---

## Section 1: The Real Daily Routine

*What actually happens, not what should happen.*

- What time do your eyes open? Alarm or natural?
- First thing you reach for — phone, water, nothing?
- What does the first 30 minutes look like?
- Do you eat breakfast? What?
- When do you actually leave for work / start working?
- What does the work day look like hour by hour?
- Lunch — where, what, when?
- When does work end? Clean cut or does it bleed into evening?
- What happens between work ending and bed?
- Screen habits at night — phone, laptop, TV?
- What time do you actually get into bed vs. fall asleep?
- Weekend routine — how different from weekdays?
- What does a BAD day look like vs. a GOOD day?
