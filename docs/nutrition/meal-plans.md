# Part 5: Sunday Meal Prep Guide

**Total time: ~2 hours.** Cook once, eat 5 days.

## Shopping (Saturday or Sunday morning)

Buy everything on the grocery list below. Estimated Hofer run: 30 minutes.

## Prep Session — Step by Step

### Phase 1: Start passive cooking (0:00 - 0:05)

1. **Rice cooker / large pot:** Add 500g dry white rice + 750ml water. Start cooking. (~20 min passive)
2. **Oven:** Preheat to 200C.

### Phase 2: Prep protein (0:05 - 0:15)

3. **Chicken breasts (1.5kg):** Pat dry. Season with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder. Lay on two baking sheets lined with foil.
4. **Sweet potatoes (if using D3 this week):** Wash, poke with fork, place on oven rack.
5. Put chicken (and sweet potatoes if applicable) in oven. Set timer 25 min.

### Phase 3: Prep ground meat (0:15 - 0:30)

6. **Beef mince (500g):** Brown in large pan over high heat. Season with salt, paprika, garlic powder. Drain any excess fat. Set aside.
7. **Turkey breast (500g):** If using pre-sliced deli turkey, skip this step. If using raw turkey breast, slice into strips and pan-fry in a non-stick pan with a tiny amount of oil (3-4 min per side). Set aside.

### Phase 4: Prep vegetables (0:30 - 0:45)

8. **Broccoli (500g):** Cut into florets. Steam for 4 min (firm, not mushy). Set aside.
9. **Green beans (300g):** Trim ends. Steam for 5 min. Set aside.
10. **Potatoes (1.5kg, if using L4 or D5):** Peel if desired. Cut into chunks. Boil in salted water for 15-20 min until fork-tender. Drain. Set aside.

### Phase 5: Everything comes together (0:45 - 1:00)

11. **Chicken is done.** Remove from oven. Let cool 5 min. Slice into portions.
12. **Rice is done.** Fluff with fork.
13. **Sweet potatoes done** (if applicable). Let cool.

### Phase 6: Portion into containers (1:00 - 1:30)

**You need 12-15 containers.** Label each with the day and meal.

## Refeed Rules

1. **Glucose-based carbs only** — extra rice, extra potato. Not candy, not pastries, not Wolt.

4. **Protein and fat stay at baseline** — do not "treat yourself" with extra cheese, extra oil, or extra meat. The extra is CARBS ONLY.
5. **Enjoy it psychologically** — this is fuel, not cheating. The big plate of rice is medicine for leptin, thyroid, and willpower.
6. **One refeed per week, on Saturday** — not two, not whenever you feel like it. Scheduled, controlled, effective.

## Diet Break Weeks (Week 10 and Week 18)

Full 7 days at maintenance calories. Different from a single refeed:

- Increase carbs across ALL meals (not just lunch and dinner)
- Add 30-40g extra carbs to pre-workout and post-workout meals
- Add 20-30g to rest day meals
- Keep the carb-timing principles (still favor post-workout), but relax them slightly
- The psychological relief matters as much as the hormonal restoration
- After the diet break, return to the current phase's calorie target — do NOT restart from Phase 1
